Coaching

Coaching is a journey of self-discovery and development.
As a leadership-coach I will help you develop and unlock your strengths and passions. This will lead to a more balanced and fulfilling life with increased energy and happiness.

I offer:

  • Coaching within Business, Career, Strategy, Stress

  • Facilitation of workshops

  • Learning and Development sessions to maximize talent and performance

  • Coaching Skills for Leaders



What is the difference between Values and Strengths?

Strengths are Values in action. The more we use our strengths, the happier we become.
We feel a stronger connection to our purpose, and as a natural consequence, we do more of the things which gives us energy.

This is a positive spiral which can help us in many situations. We become stronger, more impactful and increasingly smarter in our interactions with others, when using our strengths deliberately.



It’s effortless to use our strengths in terms of energy, the extraordinary thing here is we are energized when using our strengths.

Get to know your Values and Strengths when we identify our most important values and strengths, we uncover our ethics and our natural talents and gifts.
Having identified our values and strengths, it becomes easier to navigate our lives.
By knowing our core values, we have our inner compass to help and guide us to the right decisions and actions.



Benthe Elisabeth Joergensen (CEO) at a conference in Athens.
